Common Questions

Questions From the Field

Quick answers to what working operators ask before enrolling.

Haven't opened a textbook in years?

That's exactly who it's built for. Step-by-step coaching and AI tutoring assume no recent classroom experience — just your field knowledge as a starting point.

Do I need to complete all six papers for my 2nd Class?

Yes — all six SOPEEC papers are required for your 2nd Class certificate. This course includes all six under one subscription so you're never paying per paper.

What's included in the subscription?

All six 2nd Class papers: step-by-step calculation coaching, guided video explanations, interactive AI tutoring, adaptive practice exams, and progress tracking. Study on any device.

Can I cancel and come back later?

Yes. There are no contracts or minimum terms. Cancel anytime and your study progress is always saved — resubscribe when your schedule allows.

How long does it take to get through a paper?

Most working operators spend 4–8 weeks per paper depending on shift schedule and how much prior review they need. The full course typically runs 6–12 months.

Is this aligned to ABSA and SOPEEC?

Yes. Content is structured around 2nd Class exam objectives as set out by SOPEEC for interprovincial certification. Practice exams reflect how questions are actually marked.
