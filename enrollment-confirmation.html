How to Access the Platform

We've sent a welcome email to the address you used at checkout. That email contains a login button — clicking it is the only step you need to get in. There's no separate sign-up.

1
Open Your Welcome Email

Look for an email from support@fullsteamahead.ca with the subject line starting with "Welcome to Full Steam Ahead." It should arrive within a few minutes.

2
Click the Login Button

Inside the email you'll find a Login button. Click it — it's a one-time magic link that takes you directly into your account and prompts you to set your password. No account creation required.

3
Not in Your Inbox? Check Junk Mail

If you don't see the email, check your junk or spam folder. If it's there, open it, mark it as "Not Spam," and add support@fullsteamahead.ca to your contacts or safe senders list. This ensures every future email — lesson updates, exam tips, and communications — lands in your inbox where it belongs.
