Accessing the Full Steam Ahead Portal

Your courses, community, and subscription details are all managed through the Full Steam Ahead Client Portal. Here's how to get in.

Your Portal my.fullsteamahead.ca
1
Go to the Portal

Visit my.fullsteamahead.ca. Your confirmation email contains a direct login link — use it if you can. If you run into any trouble, follow the steps below instead.

2
Create Your Account

If you are new to the portal, click the "Sign Up" link at the bottom of the login form. Enter your full name, the email address you used when enrolling, and a password of your choice.

3
Confirm Your Email

You will be redirected to an email confirmation step. Enter the one-time code sent to your inbox to verify your account and complete sign-up.
